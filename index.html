Ambikapur's Sports Guru — 70-Year Journey

  • A veteran coach in Ambikapur, Chhattisgarh has trained players for over 10 games across decades since 1969.

Tribute

Dr. Shyama Prasad Mukherjee — 125th Birth Anniversary

  • Tribute article written by PM Narendra Modi (6 July 2026).
  • Youngest ever Vice-Chancellor of Calcutta University; linked education reform to nation-building.
  • Played a key role in keeping West Bengal with India during Partition; later opposed special provisions for Jammu & Kashmir — died in custody during his agitation.
  • Founded the Bharatiya Jana Sangh (forerunner of BJP); chose the "lamp" (diya) as its symbol.
  • India's first Industry & Supply Minister.
  • Abrogation of Article 370 & 35(A) in 2019 described as a tribute to his vision.
Policy

Hindi to Become Official Language in Assam Assembly

  • Assam Assembly will now use 4 official languages: Assamese, English, Bodo, and now Hindi.
  • Decision taken in the General Purpose Committee meeting, led by Speaker Ranjeet Kumar Dass.
  • New rule applies from the next Budget session.
  • CM Himanta Biswa Sarma clarified there is no proposal to remove Bodo.
Infrastructure

Amrit Bharat Yojana — Jabalpur Railway Stations

  • Under Amrit Bharat scheme, 15 stations in Jabalpur Division are being redeveloped.
  • 10 small stations are nearly complete; 4 more are ~80% done.
  • But major stations — Jabalpur & Satna — are lagging; work hasn't even started at Jabalpur (only tender stage).
  • Jabalpur's redevelopment cost: over ₹231 crore; design was revised multiple times, delaying the budget & tender.
  • Progress leaders: Shridham, Katni South, Sihora, Byohari — all at 100%.
Defence

First Private Missile Plant in MP — Shivpuri

  • Adani Group is setting up South Asia's largest private-sector missile ecosystem plant in Kolaras, Shivpuri (MP).
  • Bhoomi pujan done by CM Dr. Mohan Yadav and Union Minister Jyotiraditya Scindia.
  • Missiles produced here will have a 1,000 km range — covering all of Pakistan.
  • Expected to create 4,000–5,000 jobs; MP is now India's 3rd largest defence production hub (after Maharashtra & UP).
  • Adani also announced ₹500 crore investment in MP over 3 years.
Culture · Folk Art

Teejan Bai — Voice of Pandwani (Chhattisgarh)

  • Born 1956; began performing Pandwani (folk narration of the Mahabharat) at age 13.
  • Started her career for a fee of just ₹11; performed all 18 chapters of the Mahabharat.
  • Broke tradition by performing standing (khadi shaili) instead of the older seated style.
  • Honoured with Padma Shri (1988), Padma Bhushan (2003), Padma Vibhushan (2019).
  • Has performed for international audiences and heads of state, including a famous performance in front of visiting foreign dignitaries.
Positive Facts — India

India's Rural Development Highlights

  • India has the world's largest rural women's network: 10+ crore women linked via Self Help Groups under DAY-NRLM.
  • India is the world's largest millet producer — grows 38–40% of global millets.
  • World Bank cited India's Aadhaar–Jan Dhan–UPI model as a global example of rural financial inclusion.
  • India runs the world's largest rural housing scheme — 3.70 crore pucca houses given in 11 years.
  • Rural development budget has grown 211% in a decade — highest growth among major economies.

Environment · World

USA: Helicopters Restore Rivers with Tree Trunks

  • In Washington State (USA), the Yakama Nation is using helicopters to place 6,000+ large tree trunks into rivers.
  • Goal: restore natural habitat for salmon and trout along a 38-km river system.
  • Old belief (till the 1980s) was that fallen logs blocked river flow — they were removed. Later science showed logs actually help regulate water flow and are vital to river ecosystems.
  • Same trees that were cut to reduce forest-fire risk are being reused — combining forest management + river restoration.
Adventure

Khimloga Pass Conquered After 17 Years

  • Khimloga Pass (17,260 ft), connecting Uttarakhand and Himachal Pradesh, was successfully crossed by trekkers.
  • First successful crossing since 2009 — a gap of 17 years.
  • Achieved by a 16-member team, organised by trekking group Discovery Hike.
